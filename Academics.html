#Upload Section

This is the part where you, as a regular student, can contribute in the Resources Project. As long as I remain active, and
this folder
does not get whacked for no reason by the University, you should be able to see your contributions, Notes / Questions, get enlisted within the Drive Resources Folders. I humbly thank all of you who took the time to help your friends and juniors with this project.

#General Info

This section contains General Information from our university. You can find University notices (or Archives of important ones), Class Routines (and modified ones), Department Folder Links (Mostly complete for only CSE), Questions Archive, Archived Courses, and Some common materials used for Academics tasks (Assignments and Reports).
