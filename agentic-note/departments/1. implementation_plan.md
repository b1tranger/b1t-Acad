# Move Departmental Info Button & Convert Grid Selectors to Dropdowns

This plan updates the department resources page by converting the department selection grid and the semester selection grid into compact, modern custom-styled dropdown selectors. It also moves the "Departmental Info" button to be positioned persistently over (above) the semester dropdown selector.

## User Review Required

> [!NOTE]
> - The department selection grid (currently 10 tabs) and semester selection grid (up to 8 buttons) will be replaced by two dropdown selection lists.
> - The "Departmental Info" button will be moved from the dynamic course materials area and will be displayed directly between the Department selection dropdown and the Semester selection dropdown.
> - These components will be styled to match the dark, light, and gray theme configurations automatically.
